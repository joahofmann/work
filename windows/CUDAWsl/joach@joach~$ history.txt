The sustainable fix

I don't want to guess at this point because we've narrowed the problem down much further.

The sustainable fix is not patching the admin secret. It is finding where resource-dispatcher gets the old value from and updating that source. Since the admin/mlflow-server-minio-artifact secret has the annotation:

metacontroller.k8s.io/decorator-controller: kubeflow-resource-dispatcher-controller

it is being regenerated from a central template.

The next step is to identify that template (it may be a PodDefault, a manifest generated by the mlflow-server charm, or another Juju-managed resource). Once we update the source, the secret will remain correct across reboots.
